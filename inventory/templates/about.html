Welcome to e-Inventory system of Perbadanan Labuan.

unique needs and reguations of the government sector. From procurement and asset tracking to inventory monitoring

Through rigorous testing and continuous improvement, this system strive to deliver a reliable and user-friendly solution
that empowers the department to fulfill its mission with greater agility and effectiveness.
